Privacy Policy for Bryan’s Café

Effective Date: 30/10/2024

At Bryan’s Café, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you when you fill out the enquiry form on our website. The information we may collect includes:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Email Address
  • Message Subject
  • Message Body

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect from you may be used in the following ways:

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide customer service.
  • To improve our website and services based on your feedback.
  • To send periodic emails regarding your enquiry or other products and services.

3. Information Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or submit an enquiry. These security measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive information transmitted online.
  • Restriction of access to personal information to authorized personnel only.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties except to provide products or services you request, with your consent, or as required by law.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party service providers to help us operate our business and the website or administer activities on our behalf, such as sending newsletters or surveys. These third parties are not allowed to use your personal information for any other purpose.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, to request corrections, or to request the deletion of your information. If you would like to exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
